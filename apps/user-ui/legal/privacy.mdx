# Doorrite Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Doorrite ("We", "Us", or "Our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our food ordering and delivery services within Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria.

---

## 1.1 Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our food delivery service, which may include:

* **Identity Data:** Your name, email address, phone number, and password.
* **Transaction Data:** Details about orders placed, payment methods used, and delivery instructions.
* **Location Data:** Your precise geographic location (delivery address) is essential for order fulfillment. We also collect and share your general location with the delivery rider and restaurant partner for tracking and service improvement.
* **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and data about how you use our app or website.

---

## 1.2 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

* To process and deliver your food orders efficiently within Ilorin.
* To communicate with you regarding your orders, account updates, and service changes.
* To improve our app's performance, user experience, and delivery logistics.
* To comply with legal obligations under Nigerian law.

---

## 1.3 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure.
